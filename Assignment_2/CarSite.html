Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! All our certified pre-owned vehicles come with at least a 3-month warranty, and we offer extended warranty options up to 5 years.

Can I return the car if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 7-day return policy with no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Absolutely! We offer competitive trade-in values and can appraise your vehicle on the spot.

What financing options are available?

We offer financing through multiple lenders, including options for customers with various credit situations. We also accept cash and bank financing.

Can I test drive before buying?

Yes! We encourage test drives to ensure the vehicle meets your needs. Bring your license and insurance.

Do you deliver vehicles?

Yes, we offer free delivery within 50 miles of our dealership for all purchases.
